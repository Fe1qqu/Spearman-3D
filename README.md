# ðŸŽ® About the Game

You play as a spearman who fights through multiple levels,  
battling enemies and improving your stats along the way.  
On each floor, you can find items that increase your **health**, **speed**, **spear length**, or **damage**.  
After defeating all enemies, the doors open, and the ultimate goal is to **defeat the boss** on the final floor.
